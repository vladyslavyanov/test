About Us

About Us

Looking for cost effective, safe and professional vehicle transportation?

The only contact you need are us Arnold Self Drive.

We have a great customer base and are currently working with many dealerships such as the following on a day to day basis:

Lookers Honda, Marshall Honda- Seat, Marshall Volvo, Marshall Landrover, Sandicliffe, Sunwin Mazda, Co-operative Group, Robins & Day Peugeot, TVR, Gray Paul Ferarri, School Garage, Bentley Leicester and many more.

Our fully covered transporters are equipped with a hydraulic loading ramp which will cater for any make of car no matter how low and an electric winch for non starters. Our outstanding reputation has brought us work from classic car collectors, celebrities and even Royalty. Operating from our Nottingham branch we will collect and deliver Nationwide including Europe.

We can also offer a more cost effective un-covered transportation for used cars, light commercials to fleet customers or between dealerships etc.

Thank you for taking time to read about Arnold Self Drive. We would greatly appreciate the opportunity to give you a very competitive quote the next time you require vehicle transportation.
